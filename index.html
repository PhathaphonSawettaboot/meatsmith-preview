Meatsmith is the best American BBQ near you in Singapore — a smokehouse built on fire, flavour, and the iconic Southern Pride Smokers that dominate our kitchen. Guests who love the live-fire philosophy of Burnt Ends at Dempsey often find their way to Meatsmith for a more relaxed, everyday expression of that same craft. These giant smokers, flown in from the US, give our food that unmistakable bold, smoky character that keeps guests coming back for more.

Step inside and you will find a space alive with energy: an open kitchen, glowing grills, and the buzz of people digging into serious plates of meat. This is BBQ with impact: 1kg T-bone steaks, spit-roasted lamb, juicy racks of ribs, and hickory wood smoked burnt ends cooked low and slow for up to 14 hours.

"Think Big, Think Barbecue, Think Meat."

We serve American-style BBQ — low-and-slow smoked meats cooked over hickory and mesquite wood. Our specialties are 14-hour beef brisket, hickory pork ribs, wagyu cheeseburgers, whole smoked chicken and a 1KG T-Bone steak cooked on our live-fire wood elevation grill.

Meatsmith and Burnt Ends share a passion for live-fire cooking and quality ingredients, and some team members have worked across both kitchens over the years.
